Personal Lyf

          Tendulkar is married to Anjali Mehta, who is a pediatrician by profession and is the daughter of Mr. Anand Mehta, an industrialist from the state of Gujarat.
The couple has two children named Sara and Arjun. Sachin is a religious person.

             He is known to be socially responsible by sponsoring 200 deprived children per year through an N. G. O. of Mumbai called Apnalaya that is associated with Mrs. Annabel Mehta, his mother-in-law, who is also a social worker.
In 2012, Sachin was nominated for Rajya Sabha, the upper house of the Parliament of India.
